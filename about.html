Taj Web Studio is run by Leo — one person, amplified by AI, accountable for everything that ships. Here's what that means for you.

Hi, I'm Leo.

I started Taj Web Studio because I kept seeing the same thing: small businesses with premium services and invisible websites — sites that looked fine but answered nothing, ranked for nothing, and appeared nowhere when customers asked Google or AI for help.

My method is simple to say and rigorous to do: understand the business first, map how its customers decide, structure the site around that — then keep it visible month after month, and prove it with reports written in plain language.

I work AI-powered on purpose: the heavy lifting goes fast, which keeps builds affordable — and the judgment, the strategy, and the accountability stay human. My first clients are healthcare organizations in Southern California, and their families and referrers are why I take the details — accuracy, accessibility, compliance — personally.
